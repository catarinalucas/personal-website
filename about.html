— about

Born in ‘97 and raised in Viseu, Portugal I was always that curious kid that asks the purpose of everything. I wanted to know everything so I could make a world of my own. So I fell in love with both design & technology because they give you the power to change the things that you don’t like.
The dream came true when I started my Bachelor degree in Design and Multimedia. During my college years I participated in various side-projects and now I’m ready to take new steps in the business world.
