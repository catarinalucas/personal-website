best green day

BEST Green Day is an event that raises awareness for the polution problem and helps students to reduce their environmental footprint.

Branding & Graphic Design

— The event, organised by the students organisation BEST Coimbra,took place in the 15th of May in the University of Coimbra and had talks from 7 speakers, workshops and an exhibition where the companies promoted their eco products. The branding materials were constitued by posters, flyers and guerrila marketing materials.

In collaboration with:
Teresa Araújo & BEST Coimbra
