smart shopping

Smart Shopping is a project developed during my summer intership at Whitesmith which consists in a mobile application that turns shopping into an easier and funnier activity. During the intership I did both the product discovery and validation, UI/UX design and development in React Native.

Mobile Design & Development

— The goal was to develop an app that would turn around the shopping experience so we started to do user interviews to understand what were the problems that people had while shopping. After many weeks of product discovery we came to a solution with an app that would learn with the user and make the shopping list for them. We then designed and validated the prototype with more user interviews. Since I had no prior knowledge of React Native is still a work in progress.
